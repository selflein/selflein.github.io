At argo.ai I've worked on developing machine learning models and uncertainty estimation to safely reason about future actions of other traffic participants for autonomous driving.

Before that, I graduated with a Master's degree in Computer Science at Technical University of Munich, focusing on machine learning and mathematics. I wrote my thesis at DAML working with Prof. Günnemann.

Research

My research interest lie in the areas of machine learning, uncertainty estimation, and generative models. Currently, I'm interested in an "understanding-through-synthesis" approach, leveraging generative models combined with interpretable priors, e.g., 3D geometry in computer vision and robotics, or physical constraints in drug discovery.
